About Us 
   

 Lets Recycle is focused on informing kids of all the different ways that they can make a difference to help reduce waste to help the environment. Everyone can do small things that make a huge impact. Something we focus on is composting and how anyone can use it to enrich their gardens. We wish to get as many people to recycle as possible.

 Part of our campaign is to visit schools to educate students on the benefits of recycling. During our visit we cover the three Rs and how you can apply them in your everyday life. We also cover ways to reuse some of the things that you might ordinarily throw away. We also mention the benefits of composting. We focus on small changes that anyone can make in their everyday lives.
