  Reduce stands for reducing your amount of trash by only using what you need. This is something that anyone can do everyday. Reuse stands for reusing things that can be reused within reason. An example is a water bottle that you refill instead of getting a new one. Recycle stands for recycling paper, plastic, and aluminum so new things can be created with them. This keeps them from going to the landfill. The phrase helps to remind people how to reduce their trash amount.
